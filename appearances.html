Adaptations and Appearances of Miles Morales

Comic Book Appearances

Miles Morales first appeared in "Ultimate Fallout" #4 and has been a major character in comic series such as "Miles Morales: Spider-Man" and "Spider-Men"

Animated Film

Miles had a starring role in the Academy Award-winning "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse". This film brought Miles to mainstream attention with its unique style and themes

Video Games

Miles Morales has been featured in several video games, most prominently in "Spider-Man: Miles Morales" for the PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5

Other Media

Miles has appeared in various other media including animated TV series like "Ultimate Spider-Man" and "Spider-Man" (2017 series), and in merchandise like toys and clothing

Future Appearances

Future confirmed appearances for Miles Morales include the sequel to "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse"
