Miles Morales, in "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse", is a Brooklyn teen who gets bitten by a special spider.
Suddenly, he has powers like wall-crawling and invisibility. He learns he's not the only Spider-Man and has to save his city from the villain, Kingpin.
